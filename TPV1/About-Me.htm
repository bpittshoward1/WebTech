My Interests



Growing up I have always loved the water and ocean thus its was not so difficult in retrospect that my passion in life beside sports is fishing. Though I only discovered spearfising in highschool, I have been deap sea fishing my entire life. Spearfishing mixes traditional fishing with freediving and hunting. Although not as common as a tradional rod and reel fishing, it has begun to peak interest within the fishing community. It presents an active aproach to fishing and makes it, in my opinion, much hard than tradional fishing. This old style of fishing is seen as a new and innovative way to explore the ocean while allowing hunters to be more selective in the fish they hunt and ultimatly consume.
